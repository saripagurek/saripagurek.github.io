Water Puzzle

January 2015

First paint a regular 8 1/2" x 11" sheet of paper with a very basic watercolour design. Then cut into squares and glue it back together again in a different pattern.

P.S Want a real challenge? Print out this photo then cut out the squares. See if you can get them back together again in the original position (before I cut it in the first place).
